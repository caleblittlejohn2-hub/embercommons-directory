The Emberline Standard

Listed by Emberline means something.

EmberCommons is not a marketplace where anyone can pay to appear. It is a curated directory where businesses are reviewed, approved, and held to a standard. The goal is simple: when someone finds a business here, they should be able to trust it without hesitation.

01

What EmberCommons Is

EmberCommons is a curated business directory operated by Emberline Holdings Company LLC. It connects quality local and professional businesses with the communities they serve — starting in San Antonio, Texas.

EmberCommons is not an advertising platform. Being listed here is a signal that a business has met Emberline's baseline standards for quality, legitimacy, and honest representation. We take that signal seriously. So should every business that appears here.

The Standard
"Would I refer this business to my family without hesitation?" Every listing is measured against that question. If the answer is no — or even uncertain — the listing does not belong here.
02

Eligibility

Any legitimately operating business may apply, provided it meets these requirements:

  • Legally registered and operating in its stated jurisdiction
  • In operation for a minimum of 3 months, or demonstrably ready to serve customers (new businesses at launch may apply with supporting documentation)
  • The applicant has legal authority to represent the business
  • Operates in a category served by EmberCommons
  • Does not operate in a manner that violates these guidelines

Businesses outside San Antonio are welcome. Remote and national businesses may apply in the Technology, Professional Services, and Education categories.

03

What We Accept

Accepted

  • Home services — cleaning, maintenance, landscaping, repair
  • Healthcare — licensed medical, dental, mental health, wellness
  • Education — tutoring, coaching, training, academic support
  • Technology — software, IT services, digital solutions
  • Beauty & wellness — salons, spas, aesthetics, fitness
  • Food & beverage — restaurants, catering, specialty food
  • Professional services — legal, financial, consulting, accounting
  • Arts & creative — photography, design, music, media
  • Retail & shopping — local boutiques, specialty stores
  • Community & nonprofits — organizations serving the public

Not Accepted

  • MLM or pyramid-structured businesses
  • Payday lending or predatory financial services
  • Gambling or adult entertainment businesses
  • Any business operating illegally
  • Businesses with substantiated consumer fraud history
  • Shell companies with no verifiable operations
  • Businesses built on misleading or deceptive practices
  • Political campaigns or partisan organizations
04

What We Do Not Accept

The following conduct results in rejection or removal regardless of business type:

  • False or misleading information — inflated credentials, fabricated reviews, or inaccurate service descriptions
  • Unlicensed practice in a regulated profession — healthcare, legal, financial, and licensed trade businesses must hold current, valid licensure
  • Discriminatory practices — refusal of service based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected class
  • Harassment toward EmberCommons staff, reviewers, or other listed businesses
  • Reputation manipulation — fake reviews, malicious flagging of competitors, or gaming the directory
  • Illegal activity of any kind, including operating without required permits or licenses

07

Listing Content Standards

Business Name

Must be your actual registered or DBA name. No keyword stuffing (e.g., "Joe's Plumbing Best San Antonio Plumber 78244" is not acceptable).

Description

Must be accurate and honest. Do not include unsubstantiated superlative claims ("Best in Texas," "#1 in San Antonio") without a verifiable source. Do not include promotional pricing in the description.

Contact Information

Must be current and functional. If contact information changes, update it within 30 days. Listings with non-functional contact information will be suspended.

Images — Featured and Partner Tiers

  • Must be of your actual business, staff, or work — no misrepresentative stock photography
  • No watermarked or copyright-infringing images
  • No medical or aesthetic before/after images that could be misleading under FTC guidelines
  • Minimum 600×400px · JPG, PNG, or WEBP only
08

Ongoing Compliance

A listing in EmberCommons is an ongoing relationship. Listed businesses agree to:

  • Maintain listing accuracy — update contact, service area, and description within 30 days of any material change
  • Maintain licensure and legal standing — if required licensure lapses, the listing must be suspended until reinstated
  • Respond to escalated customer concerns within 14 days — documented, unresolved complaints are taken seriously
  • Comply with guideline updates — we notify listed businesses at least 30 days before material changes take effect
09

Removal Policy

Listings may be suspended or removed for:

  • Non-payment — suspension after 7 days, removal after 30 days
  • Violation of these guidelines — material or repeated
  • Business closure or cessation of operations
  • Loss of required licensure or legal standing
  • Substantiated consumer fraud or repeated unresolved complaints
  • Voluntary cancellation
  • Emberline's sole discretion where a listing no longer upholds the EmberCommons standard

Suspension means the listing is temporarily hidden pending resolution. Removal means the listing is permanently taken down. Removed businesses may reapply after 90 days if the underlying issue is resolved — except in cases of fraud, where reapplication may be permanently barred.

We will provide written notice and a minimum of 7 days to respond before removing any listing for non-payment or compliance reasons, except where immediate action is required for safety, fraud, or illegal activity.

10

Appeals

To appeal a declined application, suspension, or removal, email hello@emberlinegroup.org with "EmberCommons Appeal" in the subject line. Include your business name, the decision being appealed, and any supporting documentation.

We will respond within 10 business days. One appeal per decision is permitted. If denied, the decision is final.
